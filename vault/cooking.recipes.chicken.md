## Marinated Chicken
- 2 Tbs balsamic vinegard
- 2 Tbs Extravirgin olive oil
- Cumin
- Paprika
- Cajenne pepper
- Salt
- 2 chicken legs and one chest

Marinate 5 hours and bake in the oven
