### Ingredients
- Flour
- Butter
- Milk

Flour and milk must be the same quantities

### Preparation
- Melt the butter in a pot
- Mix butter and flour
- Low the fire and add milk
- Mix until smooth
- Keep adding milk until the desired consistency is reached
